How we work

Our approach for Reflex.

We don't make individual videos. We build content systems. Formats are the growth driver — they train the algorithm, build recognition & scale over time.

01

Kick-off & alignment

Strategic alignment with the Reflex team — goals, audiences, internal resources, key stakeholders.

02

Account audit & setup

Analyze existing accounts. Create & verify new ones. Secure consistent handles across all platforms (nameclaiming).

03

Content & distribution strategy

Content pillars, editorial calendar, posting cadence, cross-platform distribution plan, hashtag strategy.

04

Format development

3-5 recurring video formats — the growth engine. Each with a fixed structure, own look & clear purpose. Tested in pilot episodes.

05

Production & go live

On-site shoots at Reflex. Real employees, real products. Videos edited, subtitled & published within days.

06

Monthly cycle

40 new videos from 10 main assets, continuous optimization, monthly performance reporting with learnings & recommendations.
